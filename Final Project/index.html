This Day in Air Guitar History

Today back in 1983 a little known air guitarist named Sven Pickadilly, came blaring on the scene. As the tech for a band named Green Jelly, Sven was called upon to replace their lead guitarist for one night at a concert in Nova Scotia. There Sven blew away the crowd with his masterful solos and acrobatics.
